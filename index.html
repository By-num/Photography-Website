About Me

I'm a passionate photographer based in Nairobi. I specialize in weddings, portraits, nature, and events. My goal is to capture the beauty of life through my lens. I believe that every moment is worth capturing, and I strive to create images that evoke emotions and tell stories. Whether it's a candid shot of a child playing or a breathtaking landscape, I aim to create art that resonates with people. Photography allows me to tell stories through light and composition.
